Use terms

GrantStack provides first-pass screening information for business planning. It is not legal, tax, accounting, government relations, or grant application advice.

Program availability, eligibility, award amounts, timing, and application requirements must be verified with the relevant agency or a qualified advisor before relying on a report.

Paid advisory use requires human review of the project facts, official source material, agency guidance, compliance obligations, and any legal, tax, accounting, lobbying, or grant-writing implications.

A pilot memo may not be used for tax filings, grant applications, agency negotiations, public statements, board forecasts, or financial reporting unless reviewed by qualified professionals and validated against current agency materials.

Any pricing, cost comparison, ROI scenario, or consulting benchmark shown on the site is illustrative and not a quote, guarantee, or promise of award value, savings, approval, or eligibility.
